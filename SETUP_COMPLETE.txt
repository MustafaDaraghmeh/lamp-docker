COMMON STUDENT QUESTIONS:

Q: Why should I use service names instead of localhost?
A: Containers have their own networks - they can't use localhost

Q: How do I access databases from PHP?
A: Use service names: mysql_db, postgres, mongodb, etc.

Q: Why is npm/composer taking so long?
A: First install builds cache - subsequent installs are instant

Q: Can I modify docker-compose.yml?
A: Yes! Show students the structure and let them experiment

Q: My changes aren't showing up
A: Try: docker compose restart + hard browser refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R)
